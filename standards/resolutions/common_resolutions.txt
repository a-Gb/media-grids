Common Display Resolutions:

Standard Definition:
- 320×240: QVGA (Quarter VGA)
- 640×480: VGA/SD (4:3)
- 720×480: NTSC SD widescreen (3:2)
- 720×576: PAL SD widescreen (5:4)

High Definition:
- 1280×720: HD/720p (16:9)
- 1920×1080: Full HD/1080p (16:9)
- 2560×1440: QHD/WQHD/1440p (16:9)

Ultra High Definition:
- 3840×2160: 4K UHD (16:9)
- 7680×4320: 8K UHD (16:9)

Cinema Digital:
- 2048×1080: 2K DCI (≈19:10)
- 4096×2160: 4K DCI (≈19:10)

Computer Displays:
- 800×600: SVGA (4:3)
- 1024×768: XGA (4:3)
- 1280×800: WXGA (16:10)
- 1280×1024: SXGA (5:4)
- 1366×768: HD (16:9) - Common laptop resolution
- 1440×900: WXGA+ (16:10)
- 1600×900: HD+ (16:9)
- 1600×1200: UXGA (4:3)
- 1680×1050: WSXGA+ (16:10)
- 1920×1200: WUXGA (16:10)
- 2560×1600: WQXGA (16:10)
- 3440×1440: UWQHD (21:9)
- 5120×1440: DUWQHD (32:9)

Mobile Devices:
- 240×320: QVGA (3:4) - Feature phones
- 480×800: WVGA (9:15) - Early smartphones
- 640×960: DVGA (2:3) - iPhone 4
- 720×1280: HD (9:16) - Early HD smartphones
- 750×1334: iPhone 6-8
- 1080×1920: Full HD (9:16) - Standard smartphones
- 1125×2436: iPhone X/XS/11 Pro
- 1170×2532: iPhone 12/13 Pro
- 1284×2778: iPhone 12/13 Pro Max
- 1290×2796: iPhone 15 Pro Max
- 1440×3120: QHD+ - Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra

Print (DPI dependent):
- 8.5×11 inches: US Letter (300 DPI = 2550×3300 pixels)
- 11×17 inches: Tabloid (300 DPI = 3300×5100 pixels)
- A4: 210×297mm (300 DPI = 2480×3508 pixels)
- A3: 297×420mm (300 DPI = 3508×4960 pixels)
